Denver

Denver is a great city to live in and raise a family, with plenty of options for people with many hobbies. In the winter, most people enjoy skiing or snowboarding. Summers are great for hiking and biking. If you don't enjoy those activities, Denver offers excellent museums and a wide variety of restaurants from different cultures. For sports fans, Denver has a strong fan base for all major sports.

Fun facts of Denver

  • Capital of Colorado
  • Population: 729,000
  • Founded: November 22, 1858
  • Classified as an urban city
  • The average income in Denver is approximately 91,700 compared to the state of Colorado which has an average income of 92,911.
  • Denver is in the Mountain State region
