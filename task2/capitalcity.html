Denver

Denver is a great city to live in and raise a family. Denver has plenty of options for anyone with any hobby. In the winter time most people enjoy skiing or snowboarding. The summers are great for hiking and biking. If you are someone who doesn't enjoy those activities, Denver offers fantastic museums and a wide variety of restaurants from different cultures. For the sports fans, Denver has a great fan base for all major sports.

Fun facts of Denver

  • Capital of Colorado
  • Population: 729,000
  • Founded: November 22, 1858
  • Classified as an urban city
  • The average income in Denver is slightly less than the rest of the state.
  • Denver is in the Mountain State region
