Aurora Colorado

  1. Aurora has an estimated population of about 403,000 people.
  2. Aurora was incorporated in 1890.
  3. Aurora is located in the Rocky Mountain region of Colorado.
  4. Aurora is classified as a home rule city.
  5. The average household income in Aurora is approximately $84,000 compared to about $92,000 for Colorado.
