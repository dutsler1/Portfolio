Parker Colorado

Parker Colorado is a great place to live if your trying to get away from the city life but not to far into the country. Parker is a suburb of Denver and is located about 25 miles southeast of downtown Denver. Parker is one of the fastest growing cities in Colorado and has a great school system, its also known for its great parks and trails. Parker also has a great downtown area with plenty of shops and restraunts to choose from.
