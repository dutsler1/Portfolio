Parker Colorado

Parker, Colorado, is a great place to live if you're trying to get away from city life without going too far into the country. Parker is a suburb of Denver, located about 25 miles southeast of downtown. It is one of the fastest-growing communities in Colorado and has a strong school system. Parker is also known for its parks and trails, and its downtown area offers plenty of shops and restaurants to choose from.
