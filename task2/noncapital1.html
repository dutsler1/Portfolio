Parker Colorado

Parker, Colorado is a great place to live if you're trying to get away from the city life but not too far into the country. Parker is a suburb of Denver and is located about 25 miles southeast of downtown Denver. Parker is one of the fastest-growing cities in Colorado and has a great school system. It's also known for its great parks and trails. Parker also has a great downtown area with plenty of shops and restaurants to choose from.
